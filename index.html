THE HOLOCENE EXTINCTION

ANOTHER HOMO SAPIENS BY-PRODUCT

Throughout the history of Earth, there have been 5 mass extinction events. For the past 3.5 billion years, about 99% of the estimated 4 billion species that ever existed are no longer found. Homo sapiens, for better or worse, is part of the remaining remnants that are left of life.

Unfortunately, we are ourselves witnesses of the unfolding 6th mass extinction event, the Holocene extinction. Contrary to previous occurrences, Holocene extinction is a result of human activity, and not of natural causes.

The Holocene extinction has been occurring steadily for the last 10,000 years. However, the advancement of human civilization has undoubtedly artificially accelerated this evolutionary process from something that would take thousands of years to mere decades.
